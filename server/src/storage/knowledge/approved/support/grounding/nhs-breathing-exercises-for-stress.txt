Slow breathing can be a useful grounding-style tool when stress or anxiety makes it hard to settle.

A simple supportive approach is:
- sit or stand in a comfortable position
- let the breath move as deeply into the belly as feels comfortable
- breathe in gently through the nose if comfortable
- breathe out gently and steadily
- continue for a few minutes without forcing the breath

Breathing exercises should be presented as optional support tools rather than guaranteed fixes. The goal is to help someone slow down and reconnect with the present moment.

If breathing exercises feel uncomfortable, it can help to pause and try another calming technique instead.
