If someone in the UK needs urgent emotional support, Samaritans can be contacted free on 116 123.

Samaritans are available day and night for people who need someone to listen. Their service is focused on emotional support and listening, not diagnosis or emergency medical treatment.

Crisis-support content should be brief, direct, and easy to act on. Priority actions include:
- call Samaritans on 116 123 for urgent emotional support
- if there is immediate danger or a life-threatening emergency, call 999
- if urgent help is needed but it is not an emergency, NHS 111 may help direct the person to the right service

Crisis content should take priority over general support content whenever a message suggests immediate danger, suicidal thinking, or urgent emotional distress.
