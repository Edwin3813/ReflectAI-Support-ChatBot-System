If someone in the UK wants emotional support or needs someone to talk to, Samaritans can be contacted free on 116 123.

Samaritans are available day and night for people who need someone to listen. Their service offers emotional support and listening.

Useful contact details:
- call Samaritans on 116 123
- email jo@samaritans.org
- if there is immediate danger or a life-threatening emergency, call 999
- if urgent help is needed but it is not an emergency, NHS 111 may help direct the person to the right service

This support content is suitable for factual questions such as asking for the Samaritans number or how to contact Samaritans.
