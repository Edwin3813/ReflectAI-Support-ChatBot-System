If you are feeling overwhelmed or distressed, grounding techniques can help bring your attention back to the present moment.

Common grounding techniques include slow breathing, naming five things you can see, four things you can feel, three things you can hear, two things you can smell, and one thing you can taste.

Seeking support from trusted friends, family members, or professionals can be an important step when dealing with mental health challenges.

In the UK, Samaritans provide free, confidential support 24 hours a day at 116 123.

Mental health support is not a substitute for professional diagnosis or treatment. If symptoms persist or worsen, consider contacting a general practitioner or mental health professional.
